Wellington Phoenix Stage Incredible Comeback in A-League Thriller

By Marcus Chen • • 5 min read

In one of the most dramatic matches of the A-League season, Wellington Phoenix came from 2-0 down to secure a crucial 3-2 victory over Sydney FC at Sky Stadium. The win keeps the Phoenix firmly in contention for a playoff spot with just four games remaining in the regular season.

Star striker David Ball scored twice in the second half before captain Alex Rufer netted the winner in the 89th minute, sending the 25,000-strong crowd into raptures. "This is what football is all about," said head coach Giancarlo Italiano. "The team showed incredible character and never gave up."

The Phoenix have now won four of their last five matches and sit just three points outside the top six. Their next match is a crucial away fixture against Melbourne Victory, which could prove decisive in their playoff push.
