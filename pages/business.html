Wellington Tech Startup Secures $25M in Series B Funding

By Emma Rodriguez • • 6 min read

Wellington-based climate tech company GreenWave Solutions has announced a successful $25 million Series B funding round, led by prominent venture capital firm Pacific Ventures with participation from international investors. The funding will accelerate the development of their groundbreaking carbon capture technology and expand operations across Asia-Pacific.

Founded in 2020 by former Victoria University researchers, GreenWave has developed innovative technology that captures and converts carbon dioxide into useful materials for construction and manufacturing. CEO Dr. Maria Chen stated: "This investment validates our approach and will enable us to scale our solution at a critical time for climate action."

The company plans to hire an additional 50 staff at their Wellington headquarters and establish new research facilities in Singapore and Sydney. This brings total investment in the company to $40 million, making it one of New Zealand's most well-funded climate tech ventures.
