Current Openings

Senior Political Reporter

Type: Full-time Location: Wellington Experience: 5+ years

We're seeking an experienced political reporter to join our team covering Parliament and national politics. You'll break news, develop sources, and produce in-depth analysis of the political issues shaping New Zealand.

Requirements:

  • Proven track record in political journalism
  • Strong network of political contacts
  • Excellent writing and analytical skills
  • Ability to work under tight deadlines

Multimedia Journalist

Type: Full-time Location: Wellington Experience: 2+ years

Create compelling stories using video, photography, data visualization, and text. You'll cover breaking news and produce feature stories that showcase Wellington's diverse communities.

Requirements:

  • Strong skills in video editing and photography
  • Experience with Adobe Creative Suite
  • Journalism degree or equivalent experience
  • Valid driver's license

Data Journalist

Type: Full-time Location: Wellington Experience: 3+ years

Use data analysis and visualization to uncover stories and explain complex issues. Work with our editorial team to create interactive features and investigative pieces.

Requirements:

  • Strong skills in data analysis (Python, R, or similar)
  • Experience with data visualization tools
  • Understanding of statistical methods
  • Journalism background or demonstrated news judgment

Junior Reporter (Graduate Program)

Type: Full-time, 12-month contract Location: Wellington Experience: 0-1 years

Start your journalism career with mentorship from experienced reporters. Cover community news, develop your skills, and contribute to all sections of the paper.

Requirements:

  • Journalism degree or relevant qualification
  • Excellent writing skills
  • Enthusiasm for learning and developing
  • Basic understanding of media law and ethics

Freelance Contributors

Type: Freelance/Contract Location: Wellington (remote possible) Experience: Varies

We're always looking for talented freelance writers, photographers, and contributors across all sections. If you have story ideas or specialized expertise, we'd love to hear from you.
